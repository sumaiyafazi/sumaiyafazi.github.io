The Open University of Sri Lanka

The Open University of Sri Lanka is a national university in Sri Lanka. It is unique within the Sri Lankan national university system for being the only university to offer programs of study leading to certificate, diploma, degrees and postgraduate degrees up to PhD level through the Open and Distance Mode of Learning (ODL). The degrees awarded by the university are treated as equivalent to degrees awarded by any other Sri Lankan University under the preview of the University Grants Commission.

The OUSL Main Campus and colombo regional centre is located in Colombo in Nawala, Nugegoda.
There are 8 regional centers in addition to main campus at Nawala. They are:

  1. Kandy Regional Center – Polgolla, Kandy
  2. Matara Regional Center – Nupe, Matara
  3. Jaffna Regional Center – Kokuvil, Jaffna
  4. Anuradhapura Regional Center– Jayanthi Mawatha, Anuradhapura
  5. Batticaloa Regional Center – 23, New Road, Batticaloa
  6. Badulla Regional Center– No 18/1, Bandaranayake Mw, Badulla
  7. Kurunegala Regional Center– Negombo Road, Malkaduwawa, Kurunegala
  8. Ratnapura Regional Center– Hidellana, Ratnapura
